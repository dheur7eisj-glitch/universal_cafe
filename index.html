Our Story

A Cafe Born From
Community & Chai

Universal Cafe started as a small tea stall near the Shiv Mandir at Jharoda Kalan. Over the years, it grew into a beloved neighbourhood spot — where CRPF families, local workers, students, and families all come together over steaming cups of chai and freshly prepared bites.

We serve honest, hearty North Indian food at prices everyone can enjoy. Our kitchen uses fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and traditional recipes that remind you of home.
